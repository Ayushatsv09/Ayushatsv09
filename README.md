# 🌌 Ayush | Computational Physics & Software Development

Welcome to my digital laboratory! I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical physics and computer science. Here, I build interactive, browser-based numerical simulations and physics engines.

### 🔬 My Digital Physics Lab (Live Projects)

I believe the best way to understand physics is to simulate it. Click on any of the projects below to interact with my live web simulations:

*   ⚛️ **[Quantum Mechanics Analyzer](https://ayushatsv09.github.io/quantum-analyzer/)**  
    *A mathematical engine that normalizes 1D wavefunctions, calculates expectation values, and graphs probability densities using numerical integration.*
*   ☢️ **[Interactive Nuclear Sandbox](https://ayushatsv09.github.io/nuclear-sandbox/)**  
    *A database of 40+ dynamic particle simulations covering U-235/Pu-239 Fission, Stellar Fusion, and Radioactive Decay chains.*
*   🌊 **[Damped Harmonic Oscillator](https://ayushatsv09.github.io/harmonic-oscillator/)**  
    *A real-time kinematics engine using the Euler Method to numerically integrate Hooke's Law and frictional damping forces frame-by-frame.*
*   🚀 **[Space Projectile Tracker](https://ayushatsv09.github.io/space-projectile/)**  
    *A 2D kinematics simulator allowing users to visualize multi-variable projectile trajectories in a dark-mode environment.*
*   ⚙️ **[Equations of Motion Calculator](https://ayushatsv09.github.io/kinematics-calculator/)**  
    *A rapid calculation tool for classic Newtonian mechanics.*
    *   🦋 **[Chaos Theory Sandbox: Double Pendulum](https://ayushatsv09.github.io/chaos-theory/)**  
    *A visually stunning simulation of deterministic chaos, numerically integrating the Lagrangian equations of motion to trace non-linear orbital fractals.*
*   🔦 **[OpticsLab: Virtual Light Simulator](https://ayushatsv09.github.io/optics-sandbox/)**  
    *An interactive, colorful laser table simulating Total Internal Reflection, Glass Lens Ray Tracing, and the flow of photons through a Keplerian Space Telescope.*
